Arcane Magic is a type of spellcasting that grants magic users access to a variety of unique and powerful spells. These spellcasters use their knowledge of magical forces to manipulate the environment around them and create powerful effects. Spells draw on a variety of magical sources, including the power of the elements, the power of the stars, and the power of the minds of those who practice it. Arcane magic is a versatile form of spellcasting, allowing for a wide variety of effects to be created. Common effects of include creating illusions, manipulating the elements, and summoning creatures.
