Choose your bloodline, the source of your power. It has a major influence on your abilities, determining your spell list, the tradition of spells you cast, and two trained skills.

# Aberrant

Something speaks to you from beyond the stars or below the earth. Ancient and unknowable, this alien influence presses against your mind.

-   Your spellcasting tradition is Occult
-   You are trained in Intimidation and Occultism
-   You gain the tentacular limbs bloodline spell
-   **Blood Magic** Aberrant whispers shield one target's mind or your own, granting a +2 status bonus to Will saving throws for 1 round.

{{{card "spell.focus.::.tentacular-limbs"}}}

{{{featureClass "Granted Spells" level=1 id='Aberrant'}}}

You automatically add the following spells to your spell repertoire, as described in the Spell Repertoire class feature. At 1st level, you gain the cantrip and 1st-level spell. You learn the other spells on the list as soon as you gain the ability to cast sorcerer spells of that level.

-   **Cantrip** daze
-   **1st-level** spider sting
-   **2nd-level** touch of idiocy
-   **3rd-level** vampiric touch
-   **4th-level** confusion
-   **5th-level** black tentacles
-   **6th-level** feeblemind
-   **7th-level** warp mind
-   **8th-level** uncontrollable dance
-   **9th-level** unfathomable song

{{{featureClass "Advanced Bloodline"  id='Aberrant'}}}

You can gain more revelation spells from your curse by selecting the Advanced Bloodline and Greater Bloodline sorcerer feats.

-   **Advanced Bloodline** aberrant whispers
-   **Greater Bloodline** unusual anatomy

# Elemental

A genie ancestor or some other elemental influence has imbued your blood with primal fury, affecting how the bloodline spells and granted spells marked with an dagger (†), as well as your blood magic, function.

-   Your spellcasting tradition is Primal
-   You are trained in Intimidation and Nature
-   You gain the elemental toss^†^ bloodline spell
-   **Blood Magic** Elemental energy surrounds you or a target. Either you gain a +1 status bonus to Intimidation checks for 1 round, or a target takes 1 damage per spell level. The damage is bludgeoning or fire, according to your elemental type (see below). If the spell already deals that type of damage, combine it with the spell's initial damage before determining weaknesses and resistances.

{{{card "spell.focus.::.elemental-toss"}}}

{{{featureClass "Elemental Type" level=1 id='Elemental'}}}

Choose the type of elemental that influenced your bloodline: air, earth, fire, or water. If your element is air, you buffet your foes with powerful winds; if it's earth, you toss huge chunks of rock; if it's fire, you incinerate your foes with flame; and if it's water, you inundate your foes with torrents of water. For fire, all marked spells deal fire damage. For other elements, they deal bludgeoning damage. You replace any existing elemental traits with the trait of the element you chose.
