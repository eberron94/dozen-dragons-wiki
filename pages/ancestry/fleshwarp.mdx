Anguish burns in the eyes of fleshwarps, because their spirits are always in conflict. The corrupt, half-daelkyr-blooded creatures born with a natural symbiont (the symbiont is sometimes mistaken for a disfigured twin or gruesome afterbirth), then later befriended, worn, and utilized by the adult half-blood.

# Mechanics

**Hit Points** 10

**Size** Medium or Small

**Speed** 25 feet

**Senses** low-light vision

**Ability Boots**

-   Constitution
-   Free

**Unusual Anatomy** Your unorthodox body resists physical afflictions meant for other creatures. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against diseases and poisons.

# Heritages

You select a heritage at 1st level to reflect abilities passed down to you from your ancestors or common among those of your ancestry in the environment where you were born or grew up. You have only one heritage and can't change it later. A heritage is not the same as a culture or ethnicity, though some cultures or ethnicities might have more or fewer members from a particular heritage.

## Supplicant Symbiont

You and your symbiont have worked together since the beginning, fighting off disease and the weakness of flesh. You don't need to eat and can't starve. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to saving throws against diseases.

## Redundant Symbiont

Your symbiont has warped your insides, multiplying and fortifying critical organs. The DC for your checks to recover from persistent bleed damage is 10 instead of 15, and it's reduced to 5 instead of 10 if you have particularly effective assistance recovering from the persistent damage.

## Whispering Symbiont

Your symbiont whispers into your subconscious mind, altering the path ways inside your mind. You have nevertheless turned the psychic toll of your transformation into a strength. You gain resistance to mental damage equal to half your level (minimum 1).

## Cold-hearted Symbiont

Emotions are a compromise the symbiont hardly tolerates, but it refuses to deal with unpredictable nature of them being externally altered. Your emotions are dulled and hard to influence. When you roll a success on a saving throw against an emotion effect, you get a critical success instead.
