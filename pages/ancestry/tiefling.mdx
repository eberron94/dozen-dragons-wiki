Believed to be the result of infernal dealings or exposure to infernal energies from a manifest zone during birth, the tieflings within the Five Nations are feared and rejected by most.

**Hit Points** 8

**Size** Medium

**Speed** 25 feet

**Senses** low-light vision

**Ability Boots**

-   Charisma
-   Free

**Fiendish Appearance** You descend from fiends or bear the mark of the fiendish realms, manifesting as some unusual feature that belies your heritage, such as horns or a tail.
