Pathfinder 2e occult magic draws from the unseen forces of the universe and uses spells and rituals to manipulate them. It is a form of magic that draws from supernatural sources, such as spirits, gods, and other mysterious forces. It can be used to summon, control, and dismiss such forces. Through these spells, the practitioner can call upon powerful forces to manipulate the world around them. Occult magic is typically used for divination or to shape destiny, as well as to gain insight into the secrets of the universe. It is also used to access supernatural entities, such as angels, demons, and otherworldly spirits.
