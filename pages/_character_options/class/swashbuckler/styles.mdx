As a swashbuckler, you have your own distinctive style that enables you to gracefully handle everyday events and life-or-death situations alike. Choose a swashbuckler's style. This style determines the additional actions you use to gain panache and makes you trained in the skill related to that action.

# Battledancer

To you, a fight is a kind of performance art, and you command your foes' attention with mesmerizing motions.

-   You are trained in Performance
-   You gain the {{{ref "feat.skill.crb.fascinating-performance"}}} skill feat
-   You gain panache during an encounter when the result of your Performance check to Perform exceeds the Will DC of an observing foe, even if the foe isn't fascinated

{{{card "feat.skill.crb.fascinating-performance"}}}

{{{featureClass "Exemplary Finisher" level=9}}}

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a Strike you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike:

-   You can Step as a free action immediately after the finisher.

# Braggart

You boast, taunt, and psychologically needle your foes.

-   You are trained in Intimidation
-   You gain panache during an encounter whenever you successfully Demoralize a foe

{{{featureClass "Exemplary Finisher" level=9}}}

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a Strike you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike:

-   If the foe was temporarily immune to your Demoralize, their temporary immunity ends.

# Fencer

You move carefully, feinting and creating false openings to lead your foes into inopportune attacks.

-   You are trained in Deception
-   You gain panache during an encounter whenever you successfully Feint or Create a Diversion against a foe

{{{featureClass "Exemplary Finisher" level=9}}}

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a Strike you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike:

-   The foe is flat-footed until your next turn.

# Gymnast

You reposition, maneuver, and bewilder your foes with daring feats of physical prowess.

-   You are trained in Athletics
-   You gain panache during an encounter whenever you successfully Grapple, Shove, or Trip a foe

{{{featureClass "Exemplary Finisher" level=9}}}

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a Strike you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike:

-   If the foe is grabbed, restrained, or prone, you gain a circumstance bonus to the damage roll equal to twice the number of weapon damage dice.

# Wit

You are friendly, clever, and full of humor, always knowing just what to say in any situation. Your witticisms leave foes unprepared for the skill and speed of your attacks.

-   You are trained in Diplomacy
-   You gain the {{{ref "feat.skill.apg.bon-mot"}}} skill feat
-   You gain panache during an encounter whenever you succeed at a Bon Mot against a foe

{{{card "feat.skill.apg.bon-mot"}}}

{{{featureClass "Exemplary Finisher" level=9}}}

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a Strike you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike:

-   The foe takes a -2 circumstance penalty to attack rolls against you until the start of your next turn.

# Matador

> **Source** Homebrew from Pathfinder Infinite

You use a dueling cape to distract your enemies and put on a show.

-   You are trained in Deception
-   You gain panache during an encounter whenever a foe fails an attack roll against you while you have a dueling cape held in a defensive position

{{{featureClass "Exemplary Finisher" level=9}}}

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a Strike you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike:

-   The foe takes a -2 status penalty to attack rolls against creatures other than you until the start of your next turn.

# Aerial Ace

> **Source** Homebrew from Pathfinder Infinite

You are skilled at aerial maneuvers and feats of derring-do.

-   You are trained in Athletics
-   You gain the {{{ref "feat.skill.crb.quick-jump"}}} skill feat
-   You gain panache during an encounter when you attempt an Athletics check to High Jump or Long Jump and the result exceeds the Will DC of an observing foe and you do not critically fail.

{{{card "feat.skill.crb.quick-jump"}}}

{{{featureClass "Exemplary Finisher" level=9}}}

You execute your finishing moves with spectacular flair, adding special effects to your finishers. If a Strike you make as part of a finisher hits a foe, you add one of the following effects to the Strike:

-   You can Leap as a free action immediately after the finisher.
